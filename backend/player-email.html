Below are some friendly reminders for getting up and running to manage your team(s).

  • Game versions

    For the pro league, we are using Draft Day Sports: Pro Basketball 2020 version 9.13. For the college league, we are using Draft Day Sports: College Basketball 2020 version 8.20.

  • League Graphics and Files

    Our leagues use specific graphics for the team logos, players, and coaches. Make sure to download (pro/college) the graphics folder from our website. Once you download the graphics .zip folder, you'll place everything in that folder inside the graphics folder where your game is located, e.g. C:\Wolverine Studios\DDSPB2020\app\disk\graphics.

    The current league file is also available on our website on the download page. Once you have the .zip folder on your computer, unzip the content into the saves folder for your game, e.g. C:\Wolverine Studios\DDSPB2020\app\disk\saves. Then launch the program, load the SIBA association, and finally pick your coach's name and insert the password associated with your team.
